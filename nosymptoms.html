Privacy Policy

NoSymptoms is a general lifestyle app that detect nearby infection probabilities and helps you live a clean and sanitized lifestyle.

IMPORTANT NOTE: This app is not intended to address any medical diseases, nor does it provide any information or statistics about any disease.

Information Collected:

NoSymptoms collects the following information:

  1. Your Device ID
  2. Age
  3. Gender
  4. Sign up location
  5. Sign up date and time
  6. The symptoms you report
  7. If you allow, your location while you use the app or background, to calculate the probabilites with people nearby.
  8. It collects your information when you open the app or when you open the "Map" screen.
